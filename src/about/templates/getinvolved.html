Get involved.

We are looking for students and mentors to create teams & NGO partners to identify humanitarian problems.

Jump to opportunities for:

HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

1. Join a team and solve a problem.

Is there one or more SmallWorld teams already established at your high school? Contact your school representative or contact us to join a team and make a measurable impact.

2. Create a team and solve a problem.

Is there a problem in your community, or outside of it, you think should be solved? Do you want to use your passions for good? Contact us to create a team. After you reach out, we will coordinate with your high school to identify a mentor and find other students to join your team.

UNDERGRADS & PROFESSIONALS

1. Become a mentor.

Mentors are crucial to the success of a SmallWorld team's solution. The commitment and belief, as well as professional experience, that a mentor brings to his/her team is what transforms the creative ideas of passionate high school students into real-world change.

Contact us today to let us know you are interested in becoming a mentor. After the interview and training process, we will reach out to high schools in your area to match you with a team of passionate students.

CODERS & TECHNOLOGISTS

1. Build SmallWorld's platform

We are assembling a team of technologists to build SmallWorld’s platform.

We are looking for team members with any experience in:

  • Front-end web dev
  • Back-end web dev (Django preferred)
  • AR/VR web applications
  • Data visualization
  • Graphic design

We are piloting an intensive internship program specifically for undergraduates. Interns will work with an advisor and implement a unique web application on the platform. Reach out to learn more!

Contact us today learn more about joining SmallWorld's tech team!

NGOs

1. Partner with us. Identify a specific problem for a team to solve.

Many non-profits have small-scale projects, fundraising goals, or new ideas that limits on time, staff, or budget prevent from materializing. SmallWorld teams, with strong mentorship and a structured program, are capable of making tangible impacts on your organization and the problems it addresses.

Contact us today to discuss how SmallWorld teams can solve a problem for your organization.
